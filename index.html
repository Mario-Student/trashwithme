Our Mission

Every year, millions of tons of trash end up in our oceans and landfills, harming wildlife and polluting our planet. Recyle With Style is a global movement that empowers individuals to take action and make a difference.

Real Action

Volunteers collecting trash in different cities.

Impact in 2025

Our History

We started as a small group of friends in 2020, frustrated by the amount of trash we saw around us. We organized our first cleanup in a local park and were amazed by the turnout. Since then, we've grown into a global movement with thousands of volunteers and have removed millions of pounds of trash from our environment.

Looking to the Future

We want to expand globally, collaborate with institutions and create educational programs for the new generation.
